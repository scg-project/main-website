Colleen Chien


Project Director


Colleen V. Chien is a Professor of Law, empirical researcher, and founder and director of the Paper Prisons/ Second Chance Gap Initiative at Santa Clara Law School. From 2013-2015 she served as White House Senior Advisor on Intellectual Property and Innovation. She lives in Oakland with her husband, 2 sons, and pet bunny.
